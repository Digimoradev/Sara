Selected Case Signals
Case 01 / Unit Economics

Recovering lost margins through structured pricing logic.

The Situation

A founder had growing revenue but stagnant profitability. Pricing decisions were reactive, made without a clear view of true cost behavior.

The Blind Spot

Hidden cost leaks and misaligned unit economics were eroding 24% of potential margins across core product lines.

The Outcome

Reframed the financial picture to stabilize pricing. Recovered margin visibility and established a data-driven model for sustainable growth.

Case 02 / KPI Systems

Aligning scattered reporting with real-time strategic goals.

The Situation

An SME team had plenty of data but no clarity. Reports existed but weren't helping leadership make faster, better decisions.

The Blind Spot

Metrics were disconnected from strategy. The team was tracking "noise" instead of the levers that drive actual valuation.

The Outcome

Designed a custom financial control panel. Moved from scattered reporting to a unified view that aligns KPIs with long-term strategy.

Case 03 / Idea to Valuation

Building an investor-ready story from broad assumptions.

The Situation

A founder had a high-potential idea but struggled to quantify the opportunity for expansion and serious growth conversations.

The Blind Spot

Weak financial structure around business model logic and valuation readiness made the story difficult for outsiders to trust.

The Outcome

Transformed messy assumptions into a grounded financial model. Created a structured story ready for valuation and strategic planning.

Case 04 / Remote CFO

Executive-level finance thinking at an SME pace.

The Situation

A scaling business needed strategic guidance but wasn't yet ready for the overhead of a full-time executive finance structure.

The Blind Spot

Leadership lacked a reliable forecasting logic, making expansion decisions feel like guesswork rather than strategy.

The Outcome

Implemented fractional CFO support. Provided the strategic lens needed for growth while keeping the internal structure lean and agile.
